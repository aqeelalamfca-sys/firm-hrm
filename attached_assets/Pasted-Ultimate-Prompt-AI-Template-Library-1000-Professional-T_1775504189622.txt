Ultimate Prompt — AI Template Library (1000+ Professional Templates with Smart Selection & User Control)

Integrate a centralized Template Library Engine (1000+ professional, audit-compliant templates) across all modules (Pre-Planning to Finalization), categorized by audit phase, entity type, industry, and applicable standards (ISA, IFRS, ISQM, Companies Act 2017, FBR/SECP).

Upon entering any working paper section, the system shall:

Auto-analyze engagement variables, uploaded data, risk profile, materiality, and industry context.
AI shall auto-select and recommend a prioritized list of relevant templates (Top Recommended + Optional Templates) with confidence score, purpose, and applicable standards.
Display templates in a clean, filterable interface (Phase | Risk | Assertions | Industry | Standard | Complexity).

User Interaction:

User can select, deselect, or customize templates from the recommended list.
Multi-select enabled with preview option (Word/Excel structure view).
Templates marked as:
Recommended (AI-selected)
Optional (AI-suggested)
Manual (user-added)

Execution Rules:

Selected templates shall auto-populate with extracted variables, TB/GL data, and AI-generated audit content.
All templates remain fully editable until “Lock Templates” action is triggered.
System ensures no duplication, full coverage of assertions, and ISA compliance mapping.

Control & Audit Trail:

Maintain selection log (AI vs User override).
Tag each template with status: Selected / Deselected / Modified / Locked.
Ensure gap detection (if any critical template is missing, AI shall prompt recommendation before proceeding).

Final Output:

Generate fully structured, professional working paper file set (Word/Excel/PDF) aligned with selected templates.
Maintain consistent formatting, branding, indexing, cross-referencing, and review hierarchy (Staff → Senior → Manager → Partner → EQCR).

System must continuously refine recommendations based on engagement context, prior selections, and audit progression, ensuring completeness, efficiency, and regulatory compliance.
