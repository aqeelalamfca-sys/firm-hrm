2. TRIAL BALANCE GENERATION

Rule:

Closing Balance = Opening + Debit – Credit
Debit accounts → positive
Credit accounts → negative
System ensures:
∑ Debit = ∑ Credit (ZERO DIFFERENCE)
3. GENERAL LEDGER GENERATION

For each Account_Code:

AI generates:
Logical transaction volume
Dates within period
Narrations based on nature

Opening + Transactions = Closing

→ Exception_Flag = TRUE
